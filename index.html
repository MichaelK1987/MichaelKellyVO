About Me

Hi! I’m a passionate voice actor who loves bringing characters and stories to life. From energetic heroes and mischievous villains to warm, engaging narrators, I enjoy exploring a wide range of voices and styles to make every performance memorable. My biggest passion is character work in animation and video games. I love experimenting with different personalities, accents, and emotions to create unique characters that feel alive and believable. Whether it’s a larger-than-life animated role or a dramatic moment in a game scene, I enjoy diving into the performance and making each voice distinct. I also enjoy narration and commercial voiceover work, where clarity, tone, and storytelling play an important role. From guiding listeners through a story to delivering a confident, engaging commercial read, I aim to connect with audiences and communicate each message naturally and effectively. Voice acting is more than just recording lines for me—it’s about storytelling, creativity, and helping bring projects to life. I’m always excited to collaborate with creators and contribute my voice to animation, games, narration, and commercial projects.
